--- ppt/slides/slide5.xml ---
ARCHITECTURE
A hierarchical tenancy model — strict isolation, flexible structure
One SnapFlect instance serves every customer. Organizations are the primary billing and data-isolation boundary; Business Units let a partner represent each of their own downstream clients underneath a single tenant.
Platform
SnapFlect — Super Admins
Organization (Tenant)
e.g., Hexaware, or a partner agency like Virtusa
Business Unit
A partner's downstream client, or an internal division
Department / Location
Granular org segments, e.g. Engineering — Chennai
Roles & Permissions
Scoped access, e.g. a Manager restricted to one Business Unit

DEPLOYMENT MODELS
Two ways to run on SnapFlect — same platform, same guardrails
B2B — Direct Client
Example: Hexaware
SnapFlect provisions a dedicated Organization tenant

--- ppt/slides/slide23.xml ---
AI SPOTLIGHT 1 OF 3
Smarter Authoring — minutes instead of hours per blueprint
RAG Question Generation
Upload a job description or HR manual — AI drafts a full blueprint of MCQ, multi-select and essay questions mapped to competencies.
Competency Auto-Tagging
Manually-written questions get suggested competency tags with a confidence score — low-confidence suggestions route for manual confirmation.
Difficulty & Penalty Prediction
AI recommends negative marking and weighting based on semantic complexity — always advisory, author confirms before it applies.
Blueprint Balance Checker
Before publication, AI flags coverage gaps — e.g. zero questions mapped to a stated competency — inside the existing publish workflow.

--- ppt/slides/slide24.xml ---
AI SPOTLIGHT 2 OF 3
Faster, Fairer Evaluation — AI drafts, your reviewers decide
Reviewer Assist Mode
AI pre-fills a draft rubric score per criterion right inside the Manual Review screen — reviewers edit instead of starting blank.
Automated Essay Evaluation
LLM scores open-text answers against your rubric (grammar, technical accuracy, tone) and shows its justification alongside the score.
Code Evaluation Agent (via MCP)
Candidate code runs in a secure sandbox against hidden test cases; an AI agent reviews quality, complexity and security — beyond pass/fail.
Plagiarism & AI-Content Signal
Flags likely copy-pasted or AI-generated responses as a reviewer signal, never an automatic fail — protects against false positives.

Actionable Analytics — ask a question, get an answer
Chat-with-Data

Self-Healing Assessments

Cohort Comparison Narratives

ENGAGEMENT MODELS
Flexible commercial models — for direct clients and partners alike
Tiered Subscription
A baseline number of AI-processed candidates and tokens bundled into Premium and Enterprise tiers.
Best for: predictable monthly volume
Pay-As-You-Go (Credits)
Token usage (RAG, scoring) and session-minutes metered separately, billed at cycle end as credits.
Best for: variable or seasonal hiring
Bring Your Own Key (BYOK)
Enterprise clients supply their own OpenAI / Gemini key — token cost passes to their cloud bill; we charge a flat platform fee.
Best for: large enterprises with existing LLM contracts
