1. PURPOSE
The Certification module manages the complete credential lifecycle:
Eligibility    ↓Certificate Generation    ↓Certificate Issuance    ↓Certificate Verification    ↓Certificate Renewal    ↓Certificate Revocation
The module provides:
Digital Certificates
Verification Portal Support
Public Verification URLs
QR Code Verification
Revocation Management
Certificate Auditability
Future Blockchain Readiness

2. CERTIFICATION LIFECYCLE
Evaluation Result        ↓Certification Eligibility        ↓Certificate Issued        ↓Certificate Published        ↓Certificate Verified        ↓Certificate Renewed / Revoked

3. DESIGN PRINCIPLES
Immutable Certificate Records
Once issued:
Certificate
must never be modified.
Any correction requires:
New Certificate Version
or
Revocation

Public Verifiability
Every certificate must support:
Verification CodeVerification URLQR Verification

Historical Auditability
Every issuance event must be preserved.
No destructive updates.
