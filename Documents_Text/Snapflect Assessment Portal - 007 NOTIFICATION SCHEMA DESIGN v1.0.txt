1. PURPOSE
The Notification module provides centralized communication management across the platform.
Supports:
Email NotificationsSMS NotificationsPush NotificationsIn-App NotificationsSystem AlertsReminder ServicesCertificate NotificationsAssessment Notifications

2. DESIGN PRINCIPLES
Channel Agnostic
The platform should support multiple channels without schema redesign.
EmailSMSPushInAppWebhook

Template Driven
All outbound notifications should originate from templates.
Benefits:
ConsistencyLocalizationBrandingAuditability

Asynchronous Processing
Notifications should be queued.
Never sent directly from business transactions.

Delivery Traceability
Every notification attempt must be auditable.

3. NOTIFICATION LIFECYCLE
Business Event        ↓Notification Template        ↓Notification Queue        ↓Channel Provider        ↓Delivery Result        ↓Audit Logs
