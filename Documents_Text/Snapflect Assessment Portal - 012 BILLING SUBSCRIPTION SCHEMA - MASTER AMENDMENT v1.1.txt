1. PURPOSE
This amendment incorporates all approved findings from:
Billing & Subscription Schema Master Review v1.0
The review confirmed that the Billing & Subscription module is production-ready with no critical or high-severity issues.
This amendment introduces enterprise-grade enhancements focused on:
Subscription Lifecycle TraceabilityRevenue OperationsCollections ManagementPayment InvestigationSettlement ReconciliationAdvanced Tax Governance

2. CUSTOMER_SUBSCRIPTIONS ENHANCEMENT
2.1 Subscription History Tracking
New Column
previous_subscription_id BIGINT UNSIGNED NULL

Purpose
Creates a linked history chain for subscription evolution.

Example
Starter Subscription        ↓ UpgradeProfessional Subscription        ↓ UpgradeEnterprise Subscription

Benefits
Supports:
Subscription UpgradesSubscription DowngradesRenewal HistoryCommercial Audits

Foreign Key
ALTERTABLE customer_subscriptionsADDCONSTRAINT fk_customer_subscriptions_previousFOREIGNKEY (previous_subscription_id)REFERENCES customer_subscriptions(id);

2.2 Subscription Operations Optimization
New Index
CREATEINDEX ix_customer_subscriptions_org_statusON customer_subscriptions(    organization_id,    status);

Purpose
Optimizes tenant subscription operations and revenue reporting.

3. SUBSCRIPTION_USAGE ENHANCEMENT
3.1 Billable Usage Indicator
New Column
is_billable TINYINT(1)NOTNULLDEFAULT1

Purpose
Distinguishes billable usage from informational or promotional usage.

Examples
Assessment Attempts→ BillableFree Trial Usage→ Non-BillablePromotional Credits→ Non-Billable

Benefits
Supports:
Usage-Based BillingPromotional ProgramsRevenue RecognitionCommercial Flexibility

8. GOVERNANCE RULES
Subscription Governance
When:
previous_subscription_id
is populated, the subscription represents a commercial transition from an earlier subscription.

Usage Governance
When:
is_billable = 0
usage must not generate invoice charges.

Invoice Governance
When:
due_date
is exceeded and payment remains incomplete, invoice status may transition to:
Overdue

Payment Governance
payment_reference
must remain immutable once payment status becomes:
Completed

Settlement Governance
settlement_date
must only be populated after provider settlement confirmation.
