16. DATA INTEGRITY RULES
Assessment attempts must never reference mutable scoring logic.
Snapshots are authoritative.
Results must be reproducible.
Randomized question sets must remain fixed.
Review acknowledgements must be auditable.
Historical attempts must remain immutable.
