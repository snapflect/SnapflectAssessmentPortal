PART 2
ASSESSMENT DEFINITION MANAGEMENT
This section defines all master data required to build, organize, maintain, and evolve assessments.
The assessment-definition side of the platform consists of:
Technology→ Assessment→ Section→ Question Bank→ Question
All assessment runtime activities will consume these definitions but shall never directly modify them.
