ERD-A1
QUESTION VERSIONING ENHANCEMENT
Affected Entity
questions

Purpose
Support immutable published questions and controlled versioning.

New Columns
parent_question_id BIGINT NULLis_current_version BIT NOTNULLDEFAULT1

Relationship
Question v1      |      |      +---- Question v2                 |                 |                 +---- Question v3

Business Rules
Published questions shall not be edited directly.
New versions shall be created when modifications affect assessment behavior.
Historical snapshots shall continue referencing the original version.
Only one version shall have:
is_current_version =1
within a version chain.
