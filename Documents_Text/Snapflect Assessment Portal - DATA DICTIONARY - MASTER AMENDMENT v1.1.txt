DD-A2
ASSESSMENT SECTION SELECTION STRATEGY
Affected Table
assessment_sections

New Column
Column
Data Type
Nullable
Default
selection_strategy
VARCHAR(50)
No
Random

Purpose
Controls how questions are selected during assessment generation.

Supported Values
RandomSequentialDifficulty Balanced

Definitions
Random
Questions are selected randomly from the linked question banks.

Sequential
Questions are selected according to configured display order.

Difficulty Balanced
Questions are selected proportionally across difficulty levels:
Beginner
Intermediate
Advanced
Expert

Business Rules
Used when questions_to_pick is less than total available questions.
Selection must be reproducible using randomization_seed.
Selection strategy must be stored in assessment snapshots.
