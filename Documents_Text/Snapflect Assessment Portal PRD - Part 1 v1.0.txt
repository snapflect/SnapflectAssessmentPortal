1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
1.1 Product Name
Snapflect Assessment Portal (SAP)

1.2 Product Overview
Snapflect Assessment Portal is a modern, enterprise-grade, responsive web application that enables organizations to create, manage, conduct, review, and analyze technology-based assessments.
The platform provides a complete assessment lifecycle:
Technology Management
Assessment Creation
Section Management
Question Bank Management
Question Management
Assessment Execution
Assessment Review
Results Processing
Reporting & Analytics
User Management
Audit & Governance
The system is designed to support:
Internal Skill Assessments
Candidate Evaluations
Technical Screening
Employee Assessments
Certification Programs
Future AI-Powered Assessments

2. PRODUCT VISION
Create a scalable, secure, extensible, enterprise-grade assessment platform that enables organizations to accurately evaluate skills while providing a seamless experience for both administrators and assessment participants.

Improve assessment consistency.
Support long-term platform growth.
Enable future SaaS commercialization.

assessment.review
